Tinker Machines

June 2022 - June 2023    |    A Playspaces Studio's Project

During my tenure as a Software Engineer at Playspaces, I had the privilege of contributing to the development of Tinker Machines—a dynamic educational tool designed to empower children in their journey towards enhanced STEM literacy, computational thinking, and social-emotional learning. In close collaboration with Olivia Ing, the Co-Founder and CEO, I played a large role in shaping and expanding the vision of this innovative product.

My role in this endeavor demanded a diverse skill set that encompassed not only technical expertise in Python, C#, and Unity but also a range of invaluable soft skills, including creativity, collaboration, and effective communication.
